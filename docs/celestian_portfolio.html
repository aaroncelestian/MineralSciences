Named endowment partnerships — a legacy opportunity
Several research programs in this portfolio are currently sustained through endowment support, allowing them to remain independent, long-horizon, and responsive to emerging science rather than federal funding cycles. Named endowments at the Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County carry permanent recognition: the donor’s name appears on every publication produced under that endowment, in museum materials, and in perpetuity within the institution’s records. For families seeking a legacy at the intersection of human health, planetary science, and environmental stewardship, a named endowment represents one of the most direct and durable forms of scientific patronage available.
