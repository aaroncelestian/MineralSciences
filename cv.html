Aaron Celestian, Ph.D.

Curator of Mineral Sciences
Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County

Mineral scientist bridging fundamental research with real-world applications in astrobiology, energy materials, and environmental science. Recognized expert in ion exchange mechanisms and biosignature detection with extensive NASA, DOE, and museum leadership experience. Research spans from searching for life on Mars to developing sustainable lithium extraction methods, with a focus on how minerals interact with their environments and living systems to address global challenges.

Research Interests

  • Astrobiology: Biosignatures in minerals, life detection on Mars and icy moons
  • Energy Materials: Sustainable lithium extraction, ion exchange materials
  • Environmental Science: Photocatalytic degradation, metal sequestration in zeolites
  • PharmaMineralogy: Kidney stone formation, bacterial-mineral interactions
  • Cultural Heritage: Gemstone provenance analysis, art conservation
